# Deep Sea Explorer

A 2D submarine adventure game. Dive into the ocean, collect treasure, fight giant squids, and upgrade your submarine.

## 🎮 Play the Game

**[Click here to play](https://ev36251.github.io/Deep-Sea-Explorer/)**

---

## How to Play

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `W` `A` `S` `D` or Arrow Keys | Move submarine |
| `Space` or `Z` | Fire torpedo |
| `F` | Use fuel canister |
| `T` | Use teleporter |
| `P` or `Escape` | Pause |

- Dive deep to find rarer, more valuable treasure
- Surface to sell your cargo and upgrade your submarine
- Giant squids appear below 400m — and get deadlier the deeper you go
- A legendary boss lurks at the very bottom of the ocean...
